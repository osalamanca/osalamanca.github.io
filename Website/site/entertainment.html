HD Setup / Fine Tuning

High-definition television (HDTV) provides a substantially higher resolution and quality television than the tranditionally-known standard definition television. HDTV may be transmitted in various formats:

  • 720p:

    • 1280×720p: 921,600 pixels (~0.92 MP) per frame

  • 1080i:

    • (interlaced) 1920×1080i: 1,036,800 pixels (~1.04 MP) per field or 2,073,600 pixels (~2.07 MP) per frame

  • 1080p:

    • (progressive) 1920×1080p: 2,073,600 pixels (~2.07 megapixels) per frame

At a rate of 2 megapixels per frame, HDTV delivers about 5 times as much pixels as Standard Definition (SD) Television.
